Basically, e-reputation is the perception of visitors on your company, your brand or leaders.

"E-reputation is the perception that people (customers, media, competitors ...) will get from the flow of information they find about you or your business on the web. This perception is determined by the order of information itself but also by the actors of your close economic/social environment: your employees, your suppliers, your competitors, your customers… etc. Those actors compose your reputational sphere."



This e-reputation sphere is composed by bloggers, journalists and internet users who speak through various channels on the web (Twitter, Facebook, interactive media, Instagram, forums, platforms, consumer webzines ... etc). Those contributors does not necessarily belong to your close economic environment, they can have many profiles (fans, critics, polemicists, customers, prospects).

The value of perception

A company can be more efficient than its competitor but benefit from lower online reputation, it changes the game score for sure because like in the ancient Roman arenas, people can bring you to a higher success or precipitate your loss. There are multiple reasons why a business or a leader does not receive the recognition it deserves when it comes to the quality of its work, one of this reasons is the your capability to gather active communities which are dynamic enough to enhance your online reputation to reinforce your company values, ethics and quality of service.

As a matter of fact, e-reputation creates a positive environment for your business or career which conveys your values and so makes easier the acquisition of new customers or the celebration of your last work performance.

How do I know if I need to monitor my business´ e-reputation? Protect my professional life?
  • If internet users express more and more about your business/job performance
  • If at an individual level or as a brand/business you are exposed outside your own communication channels (your official website or your official youtube channel… etc).
