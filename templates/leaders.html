Ebodyguard for Leaders


Protect your profesional life

Ebodyguard for leaders is a monitoring tool of social networks and different types of sources. Based on a search engine that continuously scans various types of sources (online press, blogs, forums, microblogs, social networks, videos, photos, etc)
Ebodyguard collects for you all the search results within a single interface where you have analysis tools, you can measure the impact of your marketing actions on social networks and have a better quality of dialogue with your followers and detractors.



Watch
  • Create customised alerts about your brand, your competitors, your services and be informed in real time.
  • Collect information through a single interface.
  • Monitor all types of sources : blogs, news, forums, social networks , image sharing sites , video.
Analyze
  • Identify key trends with clear dashboard.
  • Export data and set your reports.
  • Work as a team and follow the status of ongoing actions.
discuss
  • Connect your social networks and have advanced statistics on Facebook and Twitter.
  • Measure the performance of your publications on social networks.
  • Talk on social networks directly from your dashboard.
Strategic Advice
  • Our team of experts are at your disposal to help you to build your image on the Web by creating efficient marketing actions, dossiers de press, announcements.
