Brand/Corporate Identity

The corporate identity can support your reputation or show your weaknesses

If you have doubts about the quality of your brand identity, the answer might be here!

What is a brand identity?

Is the combination of digital and print components to suit brand´s vision of itself, its personality, values and graphic concept. In terms of content brand identity takes into consideration a large range of elements such as company history, its origins, and even anecdotes.

"Identity is the way the brand wants to be perceived , as opposed to the image, which is how the mark is actually perceived by consumers ."
Geraldine Michel, brand specialist, professor at the University of IAE Paris, Sorbonne Graduate Business School.

If there is a gap between brand identity and image, it means the brand is out of touch with market´s perception, and so selling its products will be more difficult. Besides, the brand image held by consumers can reach a point at which the company has to rebrand itself or risk not bringing in sales.

What is the purpose of building a brand identity?

In a highly competitive digital environment, brand identity is at the heart of business development strategy because it enables to fix a vision of the brand which is its profile and DNA. This is the most important phase of the birth of a brand because customer segmentation and market positioning will depend of it.


How to create a Brand identity?

To create a brand identity , several elements must be worked: the name of your brand, logo, colors, elements of your customer service policy and all that constitutes the history of your brand, its origin and its past. To make your brand stronger, we work to make these different elements much more powerful, consistent and complementary with each other.

Advantages

    Enable the brand to become known and then be recognized for that identity.
    Helps to guide important decisions about the brand and ensures consistency between the various actions in time.
    Reveal the product by showcasing and telling engaging stories.
    Create innovative eCommerce moments that goes beyond the vision of well-structured catalog.
